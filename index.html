Introduction to Athboy

Athboy is a village located in the west of count Meath close to the border with Westmeath.
The Athboy river runs through the center of the village and the name Athboy comes from "ford over the yellow river"

Video Tour of Athboy

Where is Athboy?

Athboy is located approximately 65 kilometres from Dublin city centre. Located close to Kells, Trim & Navan in Meath, it is only 8 kilometres from the M3 motorway making access very convenient.
