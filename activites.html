Clann na nGael GAA

Clann na nGael is a Gaelic Athletic Association club based in Ráth Cairn and Athboy, in County Meath, Ireland. The club was founded in 2002, combining the two former GAA teams of O’Growney Club, Athboy (a hurling and football club), and An Ghaeltacht, Ráth Cairn (a football-only club).

Athboy Celtic

Today the club has over 30 senior players and over 170 juvenile players. The senior team compete in Division 2 of the NEFL. There are 16 juvenile teams competing in 9 different age categories (including 2 girls teams) in the NECSL. These teams compete in the following age groups: Youths, under 15s, under 14s, under 12s, under 11s, under 10s, under 10 Girls, under 9s, under 8s, under 8 Girls and under 7s age group.

Athboy Rugby Club

Athboy Rugby Football Club was founded in 1978 and the subsequent years have been tough but rewarding time for those who originally came together in the town with the idea of establishing the oval ball game in the area and indeed the many people who have come along since. Edmund Carroll from Athboy and local Vet Rafe Warner were both instrumental in the formation of the club which boasted, in its first year, one team and about a dozen alickadoos, The initial season of 1978–79 constituted a series of friendlies but in the summer of 1979 the club was officially affiliated to the Leinster Branch of the IRFU.

The Púca Festival

Each of Púca’s three locations in County Meath and County Louth brings something special and distinct. Athboy is steeped in the tradition of Samhain nestled next to Tlachtga. Trim is an idyllic small Irish town surrounding a stunning medieval castle, a perfect spot to create the Festival’s atmosphere of celebration and mischief. And Drogheda is one of Ireland’s oldest towns flanking the River Boyne, connecting it with the two other festival sites.

Find more information: www.pucafestival.com
Date
29th May - 1st June 2021

The Blue Jean Country Queen Festival

The Blue Jean Country Queen Festival is run by Meath Macra na Feirme and sponsored by Athboy Credit Union. Since it’s inception in 1987, the festival has brought hundreds of young women to Athboy, Co. Meath for an unforgettable weekend of fun, friendship, and memories that last a lifetime.

Find more information: www.bluejeanfest.com
